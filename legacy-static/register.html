Competition Rules & Terms

Welcome to Leaf & Lens 2026. Please read the following rules carefully before confirming your submission.

1. Eligibility

  • Open exclusively to 1st year students of our college.
  • Only one registration is permitted per submission, but teams can consist of either 1 or 2 members.
  • One entry per category maximum.

2. Originality & Ownership

  • All submitted photos and reels must be original work created by the participant.
  • Plagiarism, heavy digital manipulation, or AI-generated content is strictly prohibited and leads to immediate disqualification.
  • No stock images or previously published material.

3. File Specifications

  • Photos: Only JPG, JPEG, and PNG formats accepted. Maximum file size: 10 MB. One photo per submission.
  • Videos (Reels): Only MP4 and MKV formats accepted. Maximum file size: 50 MB. One video per submission. Duration: 15–60 seconds.
  • Both category: Exactly 1 photo + 1 video. No more, no less.

4. Theme Adherence

  • Entries must strongly reflect the theme "Green Leaf Pockets" — focusing on miniature eco-spaces, moss gardens, resilient urban plant pockets, macro nature, and green leaves thriving in unexpected or hidden spaces.
  • Generic landscape photography with no clear thematic connection will be disqualified.

5. Usage Rights

  • By submitting, you grant Leaf & Lens the non-exclusive right to display and promote your work in connection with the World Environment Day 2026 campaign.
  • You retain full copyright over your original work.

6. Submission Deadline

  • All entries must be submitted by 11:59 PM on June 3, 2026. Late entries will not be evaluated.

7. Code of Conduct

  • Respect all living beings. Do not harm plants or animals to capture your shot.
  • No watermarks, logos, or personal information embedded in the file.
  • The organiser's decisions are final and binding.

The Creative Prompt

Theme: Green Leaf Pockets

"Green Leaf Pockets" refers to small, often overlooked spaces where nature resiliently thrives — moss scaling a brick wall, wild ferns pushing through cracked asphalt, a dewdrop on a pine needle, or alpine gardens hidden in stony ledges.

Your mission: find, frame, and capture these pockets. Evoke nature's silent resilience, connect it with climate awareness, and show the world how life blooms in unexpected spaces.

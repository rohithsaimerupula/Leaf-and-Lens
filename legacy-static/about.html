The Competition

Why Leaf & Lens?

We believe a single photograph can change how someone sees the world. Leaf & Lens was born from the idea that the most powerful environmental advocacy doesn't always come from statistics — it comes from a stunning image that makes you stop and feel.

Our theme, "Urban Green Pockets", asks you to find the small, hidden pockets of nature that exist even in the most urbanised environments — proof that life persists and that we must protect it.

This is not just a competition. It is a call to action.

Climate Action

The Urgency of Now

The science is clear. Global temperatures have risen by 1.2°C. Rainforests are being lost at 18 million acres per year. A million species face extinction. Climate action requires all of us.

Your participation in Leaf & Lens is a statement: you see the world differently, and you want others to see it too. Every participant who signs the Climate Action Pledge commits to meaningful environmental action.

"I pledge to take meaningful action for the environment and spread awareness about climate change in my community."

Competition Guidelines

Rules & Guidelines

Everything you need to know before you submit

Eligibility Theme File Specs Originality Judging Prizes Conduct
01 Eligibility
  • Open exclusively to 1st year students of the college. Entries from other years will be disqualified without review.
  • Team Structure — Only one registration is permitted per submission, but teams can consist of either 1 or 2 members.
  • A student may submit in one or both categories (Photo + Reel), but only one entry per category.
  • Valid college roll number and email address are required for registration.
02 Theme — Green Leaf Pockets
  • Entries must strongly reflect the theme "Green Leaf Pockets" — small, resilient spaces where nature thrives unexpectedly.
  • The subject of your submission must strictly focus on **Green Leaf Pockets** — specifically, miniature eco-spaces, moss gardens, resilient urban plant pockets, macro nature, and green leaves thriving unexpectedly in hidden spaces. Generic landscapes with no clear connection to this prompt will be disqualified.
  • Generic landscapes with no thematic connection to the prompt will be disqualified by judges.
  • Captions (5–150 words) must explain how the entry relates to the theme.
03 File Specifications
Photo Category
Formats: JPG · JPEG · PNG only
Max size: 10 MB
Count: 1 file per submission
Min resolution: 1080px on shortest side
Reel Category
Formats: MP4 · MKV only
Max size: 50 MB
Count: 1 file per submission
Duration: 15–60 seconds
For Photo + Reel category: exactly 1 photo file + 1 video file. No more, no less.
Files not matching the required formats will be automatically rejected by the system.
04 Originality & Ownership
  • All work must be originally created by the participant. Stock images, downloaded content, and third-party footage are strictly prohibited.
  • AI-generated content (Midjourney, DALL·E, Stable Diffusion, Sora, etc.) is not permitted in any form.
  • Heavy digital manipulation that alters the fundamental reality of the scene is prohibited. Light corrections (exposure, contrast, colour grading) are not acceptable.
  • No watermarks, logos, text overlays, or personal information may be embedded in the file.
  • By submitting, you grant Leaf & Lens the non-exclusive right to display and promote your work. You retain full copyright.
05 Judging Criteria

Entries are evaluated by a panel of distinguished faculty judges across four weighted dimensions:

40%
Theme Adherence
How powerfully does the entry convey "Green Leaf Pockets"?
30%
Technical Quality
Composition, focus, lighting, colour, and production value.
20%
Creativity & Originality
Uniqueness of perspective and visual storytelling.
10%
Caption Quality
Relevance, clarity, and depth of the accompanying story.

07 Code of Conduct
  • Respect all living beings. Do not harm plants or animals to capture your shot.
  • Deadline is 11:59 PM IST, June 3, 2026. Late entries will not be reviewed under any circumstances.
  • Plagiarism or misrepresentation of authorship results in immediate disqualification and potential reporting to the institution.
  • The organising committee's decisions are final and binding. No appeals will be entertained.
  • Participants must adhere to the institution's code of conduct throughout the competition period.
